White Wine Vinaigrette

TitleWhite Wine Vinaigrette
Tagsdressing, quick

A light and balanced vinaigrette made with white wine vinegar, mustard, plant‑based oil, water, agave syrup, salt, and pepper.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 3 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 0 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: about 4 (dressing)

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • 6 tablespoons plant‑based oil (e.g. sunflower or rapeseed oil)
  • Pepper to taste
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon agave syrup

Directions

  1. In a small bowl or jar, combine the white wine vinegar and water, then whisk in the mustard until smooth.
  2. Add the salt and a generous amount of pepper, and whisk again so the mustard and seasonings are well blended.
  3. Stir in the agave syrup to taste; this adds a subtle sweetness that balances the acidity.
  4. Slowly pour in the plant‑based oil while whisking continuously until the dressing is slightly thickened and emulsified.
