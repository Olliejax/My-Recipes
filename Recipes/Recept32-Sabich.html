TitleSabich - Roasted Eggplant Hummus Pitas
Tagsvegan, lunch, dinner middle eastern

Rosy roasted eggplant and chickpea hummus wrapped in warm pita bread with cucumber, red onion, harissa, lime, and fresh coriander.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 10 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 25 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: 4–6 pita servings

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant, sliced or cubed
  • 1 cucumber, sliced or diced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 200 g hummus (store‑bought or homemade)
  • 2 teaspoons harissa (or to taste)
  • 150 g chickpeas, drained and optionally roasted
  • Large handful fresh coriander / cilantro, roughly chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4–6 pita breads
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F). Toss the eggplant pieces with a drizzle of olive oil, a pinch of salt and pepper, and roast on a baking tray for 20–25 minutes until soft and slightly browned.
  2. While the eggplant roasts, slice the cucumber and red onion and set aside.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the hummus with harissa to taste; add a splash of lime juice and a teaspoon of olive oil if you prefer it looser.
  4. If you like extra texture, toss the chickpeas in a bit of olive oil and roast alongside the eggplant for the last 10–15 minutes until slightly crunchy.
  5. Warm the pita bread in the oven, toaster, or pan until soft and pliable.
  6. Spread the harissa‑spiked hummus inside each pita, then layer in the roasted eggplant, roasted chickpeas, cucumber, red onion, and fresh coriander.
  7. Drizzle with a little extra olive oil and a squeeze of lime juice, then fold or stuff the pita and serve immediately.

Serving suggestions

  • Perfect hot out of the oven as a filling lunch or picnic meal.
