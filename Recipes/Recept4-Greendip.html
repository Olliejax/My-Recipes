Green Dip

TitleGreen Dip
Tagssnack, apero

Personal Score7,5 /10
AuthorMe ☺︎

Creamy green dip made with avocado, coriander, jalapeño, lime, and topped with pine nuts. Perfect for crackers, veggies, or as a sandwich spread.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 10 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 0 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado
  • 1 lime (juice)
  • 2 garlic clove
  • Fresh coriander
  • ½ jalapeño pepper
  • Water (as needed)
  • Olive oil
  • Pistachios
  • Salt and pepper
  • Topping: pine nuts

Directions

  1. Scoop the avocado flesh into a blender or food processor.
  2. Add lime juice, garlic clove, fresh coriander, half jalapeño, a splash of water, and olive oil.
  3. Blend until smooth, adding more water if needed to reach desired consistency.
  4. Add pistachios, salt, and pepper, then blend briefly to incorporate.
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning or spice level as needed.
  6. Transfer to a bowl and sprinkle pine nuts on top before serving.
