Refreshing tropical detox juice with cucumber, celery, lime, pineapple and mint. Ultra-hydrating with digestive enzymes.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 30 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 0 min
  • 🍽️ Yield: 1 liter (1 bottle)

Ingredients

  • 2 large cucumbers
  • 6 stalks celery
  • 4 limes
  • ½ medium pineapple (alternating for best yield)
  • Handful fresh mint leaves (20g)

Directions

  1. Wash all ingredients thoroughly under cold water.
  2. chop everything into juicer-sized pieces.
  3. Juice limes, cucumbers, celery, and pineapple (alternating for best yield).
  4. Add fresh mint leaves through juicer last.
  5. Stir well, pour into 1L bottle, and refrigerate. Shake before drinking.
