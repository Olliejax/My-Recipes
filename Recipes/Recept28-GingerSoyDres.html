Ginger Soy Dressing

TitleGinger Soy Dressing
Tagsdressing, vegan, asian
Date21-04-2026
Personal Score9 /10
AuthorAlbert heijn

A savory, slightly sweet ginger dressing made with fresh ginger, white wine vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, and agave.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 3 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 0 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: about 4 (dressing)

Ingredients

  • 1 small piece fresh ginger, finely grated or minced
  • 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon agave syrup
  • Pinch of salt if needed
  • 1–2 tablespoons water (optional, for thinner consistency)

Directions

  1. In a small bowl or jar, combine the grated ginger, white wine vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, and agave syrup.
  2. Whisk or shake well until the mixture is smooth and the flavors are well blended.
  3. If the dressing is too strong or thick, add 1–2 tablespoons of water and whisk again until you reach your preferred consistency.
  4. Taste and adjust: add a bit more agave for extra sweetness, a little more soy sauce for saltiness, or a touch more vinegar if you want more tang.
