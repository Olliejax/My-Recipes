Balsamic Mustard Vinaigrette

TitleBalsamic Mustard Vinaigrette
Tagsdressing, foodprocessor , quick
Date21-04-2026
Personal Score9 /10
AuthorMe ☺︎

A sweet and tangy balsamic vinaigrette with mustard, agave, olive oil, salt, and pepper, perfect for salads and grain bowls.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 3 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 0 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: about 4 (dressing)

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon agave syrup
  • 1 teaspoon mustard (Dijon or whole grain)
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • Pepper to taste
  • Pinch of salt

Directions

  1. In a foodprocessor combine the balsamic vinegar, agave syrup, mustard, salt, and pepper.
  2. mix until the mixture is smooth and well blended.
  3. Slowly pour in the olive oil while whisking continuously, until the dressing has thickened slightly and is emulsified.
  4. Taste and adjust: add a bit more balsamic vinegar for extra tang, a little more agave if you want it sweeter, or an extra pinch of salt and pepper if needed.
