Green Spice Juice

TitleGreen Spice Juice
Tagsjuice, detox
Date2026-04-10

AuthorMe ☺︎

Spicy green detox juice packed with leafy greens, celery, tomato and a jalapeno kick. Powerful cleansing and alkalizing blend.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 30 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 0 min
  • 🍽️ Yield: 1 liter (1 bottle)

Ingredients

  • 1 large lemon
  • 4 cups fresh spinach (large handful)
  • 8 sprigs fresh parsley
  • 8 stalks celery
  • 1¼ small jalapeño peppers (or 1 for mild)
  • 4 medium tomatoes
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 cups ice

Directions

  1. Wash all vegetables thoroughly under cold water.
  2. Juice lemon, spinach, parsley, celery, jalapeño, and tomatoes in this order.
  3. Stir in salt until dissolved.
  4. Pour over ice in 1L bottle and shake well.
  5. Store refrigerated, shake before drinking. Best within 48 hours.
