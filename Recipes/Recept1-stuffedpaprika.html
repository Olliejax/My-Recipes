Stuffed Peppers with Falafel

TitleStuffed Peppers with Falafel

Author Do Not Remember ☹︎

Healthy stuffed peppers with quinoa, orange, and falafel, a light but filling lunch or party dish.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 20 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 30 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 (yellow) bell peppers
  • 250 grams quinoa or couscous
  • 1 fennel bulb
  • 2 oranges or mandarins
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • 3 spring onions
  • 1 bunch mint
  • 300 grams falafel
  • 200 grams baby spinach
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons hummus

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Remove the top of the peppers and a small piece from the bottom so they stand upright. Discard the seeds and rinse the peppers clean. Place them upright in a baking dish, sprinkle lightly with olive oil, and bake for about 15 minutes until they are soft and have a golden‑brown edge.
  3. In the meantime, prepare the quinoa or couscous according to the package instructions. You can add a bit of beet juice if you want the quinoa to turn pink.
  4. Finely chop the cucumber, spring onions, fennel, and 3 sprigs of mint. Zest the oranges and set the zest aside. Then cut the orange segments into pieces.
  5. Let the quinoa or couscous cool slightly, then transfer it to a bowl. Add the cucumber, spring onions, mint, capers, fennel, orange zest, and orange segments. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Heat a bit of oil in a frying pan and fry the falafel balls until golden brown.
  7. Remove the peppers from the oven and fill them with the quinoa mixture. Drizzle a little olive oil on top.
  8. Take a plate, arrange a bed of baby spinach, place the stuffed peppers on top, and serve with the falafel balls and hummus.
