Avocado with Rice Vinegar Dressing

A simple avocado dish dressed with rice vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper, great as a light salad or topping.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 2 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 0 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 theespoon olive oil
  • Pepper to taste
  • Pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and slice or cube the flesh into a closeable jar.
  2. Add all the other ingredient in to the jar.
  3. Shake the jar until all ingredients are well combined and the avocado is smooth.
  4. Add additional water for the prefered consistency.
