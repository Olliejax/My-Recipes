Tofu Scramble with Spinach

A fluffy, savory tofu scramble with spinach, onion, garlic, and spices, served with basil, black olives, and sun‑dried tomatoes.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 10 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 15 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: 2–3

Ingredients

  • 300 grams firm tofu
  • 100 grams fresh spinach
  • 1 white onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 0.5 teaspoon turmeric
  • 0.5 teaspoon paprika powder
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Slice of bread

Directions

  1. Drain the tofu and press out excess liquid, then crumble it with a fork into small curds.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a non‑stick frying pan over medium heat. Add the finely chopped onion and garlic, and cook until soft and fragrant.
  3. Stir in the tomato paste and cook for 1–2 minutes so the flavor deepens.
  4. Add the crumbled tofu to the pan and sprinkle with turmeric, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well so the tofu turns a rich yellow color.
  5. Add the spinach and stir until it wilts into the scramble.
  6. Cook for another 4–6 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the tofu is heated through and the mixture is slightly crispy in parts.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning with extra soy sauce, salt, or pepper if needed.
  8. Plate the tofu scramble and finish with fresh basil, sliced black olives, and chopped sun‑dried tomatoes.

Serving suggestions

  • Enjoy with toast, tortilla, or a green salad.
  • Can be served for breakfast or as a light lunch.
