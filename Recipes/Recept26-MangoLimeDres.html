Mango Lime Mustard Dressing

TitleMango Lime Mustard Dressing

AuthorMe ☺︎

A sweet and tangy dressing made with mango, lime, mustard, rice vinegar, plant‑based oil, and salt, perfect for tropical salads and grain bowls.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 5 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 0 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: about 4 (dressing)

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe mango, flesh diced
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1 teaspoon mustard (Dijon or whole grain)
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 4 tablespoons plant‑based oil (e.g. sunflower or rapeseed oil)
  • Pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Peel and dice the mango, then add it to a blender or food processor.
  2. Squeeze the lime over the mango and add the mustard, rice vinegar, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Pulse or blend until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
  4. With the blender running, gradually pour in the plant‑based oil until the dressing thickens slightly and emulsifies.
  5. Taste and adjust: add a bit more lime juice for extra tang, a touch more oil if it’s too sharp, or a little more salt if needed.
