Vegan Vol‑au‑Vent

A creamy vegan vol‑au‑vent filling with Beyond Sausage balls, plant‑based chicken, chestnut mushrooms, parsley, and a light nutmeg‑spiced béchamel‑style sauce.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 20 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 40 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: 4–6

Ingredients

Vegetarian chicken & mushrooms
  • 320 g plant‑based chicken pieces
  • 250 g chestnut mushrooms, quartered
  • 30–40 g plant‑based butter (2–3 knobs, divided)
  • Pepper and salt to taste
Beyond Sausage balls
  • 2 Beyond Sausage links
  • 1 L water
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable bouillon powder
Sauce
  • 4 tablespoons plant‑based butter (28 g total, for roux)
  • 2 tablespoons flour (about 20 g)
  • 200 ml plant‑based cream
  • All the cooking broth from the sausage balls (about 1 L minus evaporation)
  • Grated nutmeg, salt, and pepper
Finishing
  • Large handful fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 small sheet puff pastry (vegan, for vol‑au‑vent or as an open topping)

Directions

  1. In a small saucepan, bring 1 L water with 3 tablespoons vegetable bouillon powder to a boil. Lower the heat to a gentle simmer.
  2. Peel the skin off the Beyond Sausages, then pinch off small pieces and roll them into balls until all the sausage is used.
  3. Gently drop the balls into the simmering bouillon. When they float to the top, simmer for 5 more minutes, then remove with a slotted spoon and set aside in a bowl (do not discard the broth).
  4. In a larger pot, melt a knob of plant‑based butter over medium heat. Add the quartered chestnut mushrooms and sauté until soft and slightly browned, seasoning with salt and pepper.
  5. Remove the mushrooms from the pot and set aside with the balls. In the same pot, add another knob of butter and sear the plant‑based chicken pieces until nicely browned, seasoning with more salt and pepper.
  6. Remove the chicken and keep it with the mushrooms and balls.
  7. To make the roux: add 4 tablespoons plant‑based butter to the same pot and let it melt. Stir in the flour and cook for 2–3 minutes on low heat until the mixture smells slightly like cookies and the raw flour taste is gone.
  8. Slowly add the bouillon from the sausage balls, a ladle at a time, whisking constantly until the sauce thickens. Add all the broth and simmer until you have a smooth, lightly thickened base.
  9. Stir in the plant‑based cream, bring back to a gentle simmer, and cook for 3–5 minutes without boiling hard. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of ground nutmeg.
  10. Return the sausage balls, “chicken” pieces, and mushrooms to the pot. Stir gently to combine and let everything warm through. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  11. Stir in the finely chopped parsley and let the pot rest off the heat for 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  12. While you prep the pastry: pre‑bake a vegan puff‑pastry vol‑au‑vent base (or a small sheet in a casserole) until golden and crisp.
  13. Transfer the warm filling into the vol‑au‑vent container or place the baked puff‑pastry sheet on top of the serving dish to create a “casserole”‑style vegan vol‑au‑vent. Serve immediately.

Serving suggestions

  • Great with a side of potatoes or a light green salad.
  • Excellent reheated the next day, as the sauce becomes even richer.
