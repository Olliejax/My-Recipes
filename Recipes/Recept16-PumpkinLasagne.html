Pumpkin Lasagne

TitlePumpkin Lasagne
Tagsvegan, dinner, lasagne, pumpkin
Date2026-04-10
Personal Score9 /10
AuthorMe ☺︎

Layered pumpkin lasagne with vegetable slices, basil tomato sauce, Italian herbs, pumpkin leaves, and creamy cashew cheese sauce.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 30 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 40 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • Carrot slices
  • Bell pepper
  • Zucchini
  • Basil (for pasta sauce)
  • Italian herbs
  • Pumpkin leaves
  • Plant-based cheese
  • Soaked cashew nuts
  • Nutritional yeast
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Water
  • Soy milk
  • Pepper and salt

Directions

  1. Thinly slice carrot, bell pepper, and zucchini. Prepare basil tomato sauce with Italian herbs.
  2. Soak cashew nuts for 4+ hours or boil 15 minutes. Drain.
  3. Blend cashews, nutritional yeast, garlic, lemon juice, soy milk, water, salt, and pepper until smooth to make cashew cheese sauce.
  4. Preheat oven to 180°C. Layer in baking dish: tomato sauce, vegetable slices, pumpkin leaves, cashew sauce, plant-based cheese. Repeat.
  5. Bake 35-40 minutes until vegetables tender and cheese melted.
  6. Let rest 10 minutes before serving.

Variations

You can customize the lasagne with different vegetables and seasonings.

  • → Spinach lasagne: Add spinach between layers for extra greens.
  • → Mushroom lasagne: Slice mushrooms and layer with the zucchini.
  • → Spicy lasagne: Add chili flakes to the tomato sauce.
