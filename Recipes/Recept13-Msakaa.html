Soft aubergine and chickpeas cooked in a rich tomato sauce with onion, garlic, and warm spices. Delicious with naan bread and hummus.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 20 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 35 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes (peeled)
  • Onion
  • Aubergine
  • Garlic
  • Chickpeas
  • Naan bread
  • Hummus
  • Spices

Directions

  1. Cut the aubergine into cubes, sprinkle with salt, and let it sit for 10–15 minutes. Pat dry.
  2. Chop the onion and garlic. Peel the tomatoes and cut into small pieces.
  3. Heat oil in a large pan and sauté the onion until soft. Add garlic and spices and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook down into a sauce.
  5. Add aubergine and chickpeas, then simmer until aubergine is soft and sauce thickens.
  6. Serve warm with naan bread and hummus.
