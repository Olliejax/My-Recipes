Vegetable Chickpea Tagine

TitleVegetable Chickpea Tagine

AuthorMe ☺︎

A warm and aromatic Moroccan-style vegetable tagine with chickpeas, spices, and fresh herbs.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 15 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 35 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 fresh tomatoes, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 can (400 g) diced tomatoes
  • 200 ml water
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 150 grams green peas
  • 1 can (240 g drained) chickpeas
  • 1 handful fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon paprika powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt and pepper
  • 0.5 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ras el hanout
  • 0.5 teaspoon ground ginger

Directions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan or tagine over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until soft and translucent.
  2. Add the garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the fresh tomatoes, red bell pepper, and canned diced tomatoes. Mix well.
  4. Add the carrots, chickpeas, peas, and water. Stir to combine.
  5. Season with paprika, salt, pepper, turmeric, ras el hanout, and ground ginger.
  6. Cover and let simmer on low heat for about 25–30 minutes, until the vegetables are tender and the flavors have developed.
  7. Stir in the fresh parsley just before serving.
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Serve warm, optionally with couscous or bread.

Garnish

  • 1 handful pine nuts (lightly toasted)
  • 1 handful fresh parsley or coriander
