A fresh and colorful fruit salad with apples, pears, mango, berries, pomegranate seeds, and mint.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 20 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 0 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 3 apples, cored and sliced
  • 4 pears, cored and sliced
  • 1 mango, peeled and diced
  • 100 grams blueberries
  • 1 pomegranate, seeds only
  • Large handful fresh mint leaves, roughly chopped
  • 100 grams raspberries

Directions

  1. Wash and dry all the fruit.
  2. slice them in preferable sizes.
  3. serve inn one big bowl and mix.

Serving suggestions

    for longer freshness, add a squeeze of lemon juice to the fruit salad to prevent browning and enhance the flavors.
