Tahini Lemon Dressing

TitleTahini Lemon Dressing
Tagsdressing, quick

AuthorMe ☺︎

A creamy, tangy tahini dressing made with lemon, tahini, water, salt, and pepper, perfect for salads, bowls, or as a dip.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 3 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 0 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: about 4 (dressing)

Ingredients

  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 3 tablespoons tahini
  • 3–4 tablespoons water
  • Pepper to taste
  • Pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Squeeze the lemon to get fresh lemon juice and add it to a small bowl.
  2. Add the tahini and whisk until the mixture thickens and the tahini loosens.
  3. Gradually add the water, a tablespoon at a time, whisking well after each addition until the dressing reaches your preferred consistency.
  4. Season with a pinch of salt and freshly ground pepper, then taste and adjust; add more lemon juice for extra tang or a bit more water if it’s too thick.
