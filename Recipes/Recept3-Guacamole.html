Guacamole

Creamy guacamole made with ripe avocados, lime, garlic, chili, shallots, and tomato. Great with tortilla chips, bread, or as a topping.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 10 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 0 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • 1 red chili pepper
  • 1 lime
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Shallots
  • 1 tomato
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Finely chop the garlic place in a bowl.
  2. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork until it is as smooth or chunky as you like.
  4. Finely chop the red chili pepper, shallots and tomato.
  5. Add the chopped ingredients to the avocado.
  6. Squeeze in the lime juice and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Mix everything together, taste, and adjust the seasoning if needed before serving.

Variations

You can turn the same basic hummus into many fun flavors by adding different spices and vegetables.

  • → Sweet mango guacamole: add mango and a bit of coriander.
  • → Indian spiced guacamole: Incorporate ginger, green chillies and a hint of umin or garam masala.
