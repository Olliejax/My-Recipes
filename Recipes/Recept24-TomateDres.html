Tomato Basil Vinaigrette

A fresh tomato dressing made with cherry tomatoes, olive oil, basil, red wine vinegar, shallot, salt, and pepper.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 5 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 0 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: about 4 (dressing)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved or quartered
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1 small shallot, finely minced
  • Pepper to taste
  • Pinch of salt

Directions

  1. In a foodprocessor, combine the shallot and red wine vinegar and let sit for 1–2 minutes so the shallot softens and the flavors meld.
  2. Add the olive oil, salt, and pepper, then whisk until the mixture is slightly thickened and emulsified.
  3. Stir in the chopped basil and the cherry tomatoes (cut side up if you like them more “chunky”).
  4. Taste and adjust: add a bit more vinegar for extra tang, a pinch more salt, or a little more olive oil if it’s too sharp.
  5. For a thinner dressing, mash some of the tomatoes or stir with a fork to release more juice.
