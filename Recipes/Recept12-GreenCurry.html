Green Curry

TitleGreen Curry
Tagsvegan, dinner, curry, spicy
Date2026-04-10
Personal Score9 /10
AuthorMe ☺︎

Creamy vegan green curry with coconut milk, fresh herbs, broccoli, peas, and vegan shawarma. Fragrant, spicy, and full of fresh flavor.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 20 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 20 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 white onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 piece fresh ginger
  • Fresh coriander
  • 1 green chili pepper
  • Peas
  • Spring onion
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • Broccoli
  • Green asparagus
  • Snow peas
  • 1 red chili pepper
  • 1 lime
  • Fresh basil
  • Optional with vegan shawarma or themphe and additional vegetables: bell peppers, zucchini

Directions

  1. Finely chop the onion, garlic, ginger, coriander stems, and green chili pepper.
  2. Heat a little oil in a large pan or wok and sauté the onion, garlic, ginger, and green chili until soft and fragrant.
  3. Add the coconut milk and stir well to create the sauce.
  4. Add the broccoli, snow peas, peas, and any optional vegetables such as green asparagus. Let everything simmer until the vegetables are tender but still bright.
  5. Add the vegan shawarma and let it heat through in the sauce.
  6. Finish with sliced spring onion, red chili, lime juice, fresh coriander, and basil. Taste and adjust with extra lime or salt if needed.
