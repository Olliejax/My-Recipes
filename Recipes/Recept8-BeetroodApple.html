Beet Apple Juice

Sweet and earthy red beet juice mixed with apple juice. Powerful nitrate source for stamina and circulation.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 30 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 0 min
  • 🍽️ Yield: 1 bottles

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large red beet
  • apples

Directions

  1. Peel and chop beet into juicer-sized chunks.
  2. Juice the beet (add ginger if using).
  3. Mix beet juice with apple juice.
  4. Serve immediately over ice or store refrigerated up to 24h.
