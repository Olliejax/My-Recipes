Smoky roasted eggplant dip with tahini, lemon, cumin, garlic, and fresh coriander. Beautifully garnished with pomegranate seeds.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 15 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 45 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 eggplants
  • 1 tsp tahini
  • ½ lemon (juice)
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2 small garlic cloves
  • Fresh coriander, finely chopped
  • Pomegranate seeds, for garnish
  • Salt, to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Prick eggplants with a fork and roast whole on a baking sheet for 40-45 minutes until very soft.
  2. Let eggplants cool slightly, then cut in half lengthwise and scoop out the flesh into a bowl (discard skin).
  3. Mash the eggplant flesh with a fork until mostly smooth but with some texture remaining.
  4. Finely mince the garlic cloves and add to the eggplant along with tahini, lemon juice, cumin, and a pinch of salt.
  5. Mix well and adjust seasoning - add more lemon juice or salt if needed.
  6. Transfer to serving bowl, sprinkle with finely chopped coriander and pomegranate seeds. Serve with pita or vegetables.
