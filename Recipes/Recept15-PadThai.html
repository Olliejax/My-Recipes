Pad Thai

TagsNoodles, dinner, Thaï, Tofu

Sweet, salty, sour, and spicy Pad Thai with noodles, tofu, vegetables, peanuts, and a rich peanut-based sauce. Optional shrimp and egg can be added for a non-vegan version.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 20 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 15 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: 4

Ingredients

For the wok:
  • Broccoli
  • Red bell pepper
  • Tofu
  • Peanuts
  • Coriander
  • Spring onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • Noodles of choice
For the sauce:
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sriracha
  • 4 tsp brown sugar
  • 3 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
Non-vegan additions:
  • Shrimp
  • Egg

Directions

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Mix the soy sauce, sriracha, brown sugar, rice vinegar, and peanut butter in a bowl to make the sauce.
  3. Heat oil in a wok or large pan and sauté the garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add broccoli, red bell pepper, and tofu. Stir-fry until the vegetables are tender and the tofu is lightly golden.
  5. If using shrimp or egg, cook them in the pan at this stage.
  6. Add the noodles and sauce, then toss everything together until well coated.
  7. Finish with spring onion, coriander, and chopped peanuts before serving.
