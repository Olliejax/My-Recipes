Fluffy Spelt Pancakes

TitleFluffy Spelt Pancakes

Light, fluffy spelt pancakes made with whole spelt flour, almond milk, coconut blossom sugar, baking powder, plant‑based oil, baking soda, and apple cider vinegar.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 10 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 15 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: about 8–10 pancakes

Ingredients

  • 300 g spelt flour
  • 2 tablespoons coconut blossom sugar (or any plant‑based sugar)
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 420 g almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon plant‑based oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tablespoon baking soda (add just before the last step)
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar (add last)

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the spelt flour, coconut blossom sugar, baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  2. In a separate bowl or measuring cup, combine the almond milk, plant‑based oil, and apple cider vinegar. Let sit for 1–2 minutes to form a light “buttermilk” mixture.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir gently until just combined; do not overmix, a few lumps are fine.
  4. Right before cooking, add the baking soda and mix very gently and briefly to keep the batter airy.
  5. Heat a non‑stick frying pan or griddle over medium‑low heat and lightly oil the surface.
  6. Use a ladle or measuring cup to pour the batter onto the pan in small rounds, leaving space between them.
  7. When small bubbles appear on the surface and the edges start to look set, flip the pancakes and cook the other side until golden brown.
  8. Repeat until all the batter is used, keeping cooked pancakes warm if needed.
  9. Serve warm with maple syrup, fresh fruit, or a dollop of nut butter.

Notes

  • The apple cider vinegar and baking soda create extra lift, so mix the batter very gently and avoid over‑mixing.
  • For a thinner batter, add a splash of extra almond milk; for thicker pancakes, let the batter rest for 5 minutes before starting to cook.
