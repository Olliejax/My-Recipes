Potent immunity-boosting gingershots combining fresh turmeric, lemon, ginger and black pepper for the absorption of turmeric.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 60 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 0 min
  • 🍽️ Yield: 4 bottles

Ingredients

  • 3 kg lemons
  • 1.28 kg fresh turmeric root (320g per bottle × 4)
  • 2-3 knobs black pepper
  • rest ginger

Directions

  1. Wash lemons and turmeric thoroughly.
  2. Chop lemons and turmeric into juicer-sized pieces.
  3. Run all lemons, turmeric, and black pepper through centrifugal juicer.
  4. Strain the juice through a fine mesh sieve and place into sterilized bottles.
  5. Store refrigerated and shake before each use.
