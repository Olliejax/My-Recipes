Tomato and Basil Puff Pastry Tart

A simple and flavorful puff pastry tart with sweet cherry tomatoes, basil, shallot, garlic, and toasted pine nuts.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 15 min
  • 🍳 Cook time: 25 min
  • 🍽️ Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • Colored cherry tomatoes
  • 1 shallot
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil
  • Pine nuts
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (390°F).
  2. Roll out the puff pastry on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
  3. Thinly slice the shallot and garlic, and halve the cherry tomatoes.
  4. Spread the shallot and garlic over the puff pastry, then add the tomatoes on top.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, until the pastry is golden and crisp.
  7. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves and toasted pine nuts.
  8. Serve warm or at room temperature.
