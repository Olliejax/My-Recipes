Raspberry Cava Sorbet

TitleRaspberry Cava Sorbet

A bright, sparkling raspberry sorbet made with raspberries, sparkling wine (cava), strawberries, mint, and served with a crunchy chocolate‑flavored biscuit.

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 10 min
  • 🧊 Freezing time: 4–6 h
  • 🍽️ Servings: 4

Ingredients

Total recipe
  • 1 or 2 scoops rasbarry Sorbet (made or bought)
  • a splash of cava
  • mint leaves
  • fresh strawberries, sliced (optional)
  • something crunchy (optional)
For the sorbet:
  • 500 grams raspberries (fresh or frozen)
  • 200 ml water
  • 200 grams sugar
  • 1 sheet of gelatin (or a vegan alternative like agar‑agar)
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Directions

Make the sorbet:
  1. If you’re using frozen raspberries, take them out of the freezer about half an hour beforehand so they can thaw slightly.
  2. Soak the gelatin sheet in cold water for about 5 minutes until it softens.
  3. In a heavy-bottomed saucepan, combine the water and sugar. Heat over medium heat, stirring until the sugar has completely dissolved.
  4. Once the sugar has dissolved, squeeze out the excess water from the gelatin sheet and stir it into the hot sugar syrup until fully dissolved.
  5. Place the raspberries in a blender and gradually pour in the sugar syrup while blending until smooth.
  6. Finally, stir in the lemon juice to brighten the flavor.
  7. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions, usually for about 30–50 minutes until it reaches a sorbet consistency.
Assemble the sorbet:
  1. Slice the strawberries and mint in preferred sizes.
  2. Scoop the sorbet in a bowl or glass.
  3. Pour the cava over the sorbet.
  4. Add the strawberries, mint, and crunch.
